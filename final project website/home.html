Welcome to Mind Stream Therapy

The main objective of Mind Stream therapy is to give critical and easy access to therapy for content creators.

Our services will allow content creators to push past obstacles they are facing while making content.

A short example of such obstacles are lack of communication, anxiety, depression, bad influences, and creativity drought.

Traditional mental health care is too slow, too expensive, and doesn't relate to the struggles of living in a digital world.

We aim for affordable mental health care, inclusivity for all.... and the most important part accessibility!!
